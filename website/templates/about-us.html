About Us

Northern Chinese cuisine, as a unique flavor genre, is famous for its unique connotation of the rough and thick taste of the northern nation.

Rice is the main food, with dumplings, steamed buns, flower rolls, and various flatbreads as the staple food. Pickled cabbage is also an indispensable dish on the table of Northeasterners who are warm and hospitable in the cold northern winter. Northeastern cuisine with thick lines, not sticking to details, is quite like a bold Northeasterner, which is very appetizing.

Northeastern cuisine is mainly stewed, with strong color and strong flavor.
